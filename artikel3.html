International Volunteering

7th August 2022

With great honor and enthusiasm, I volunteered as the Liaison Officer for Luxembourg at the highly anticipated International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) held in Yogyakarta 2023. Immersed in a melting pot of global talent, I played a pivotal role in facilitating smooth communication and cultural exchange among participants from different nations.

Embracing the spirit of collaboration, I acted as a bridge between the Luxembourg contestants and the local community, forging connections and encouraging meaningful interactions. From assisting with language barriers to providing guidance on local customs, I strived to create a memorable experience for everyone involved. Witnessing the friendships formed and the bonds forged across borders was truly rewarding.

As I reflect on this remarkable journey, I am filled with gratitude for the opportunity to contribute to such a prestigious event. The memories, friendships, and lessons learned will forever hold a special place in my heart. This experience has inspired me to continue embracing volunteerism, cultural exchange, and the pursuit of excellence in every endeavor I undertake.

Alif Ridwan
