Japanese Certification Exam

12th January 2023

In a remarkable feat of determination and perseverance, I, a non-literature student, undertook the challenge of conquering the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N5 level. Through diligent study and unwavering dedication, I successfully passed the examination, exceeding all expectations.

Through countless hours of self-study, practice exams, and immersion in the language, I gradually gained a deeper understanding of Japanese grammar structures and expanded my vocabulary repertoire. Additionally, my determination to excel pushed me to master the intricate strokes of kanji characters, enabling me to decipher and comprehend written texts more effectively.



As I reflect on this achievement, I am filled with gratitude for the journey I undertook and the personal growth it has brought. I hope my story serves as an inspiration for others to pursue their dreams, regardless of their field of study, and embrace the joy and fulfillment that comes from surpassing their own expectations.

Alif Ridwan
