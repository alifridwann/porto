ARMS Production House, a renowned film production company, enthusiastically joined the film competition organized by TVRI (Television Station) with a compelling theme centered around the influential figure of Sukarno. This event provided a remarkable opportunity for ARMS Production House to showcase their filmmaking prowess and pay tribute to the remarkable legacy of Indonesia's first president.



As ARMS Production House embarked on this creative endeavor, the team delved deep into research, meticulously studying Sukarno's life, ideologies, and significant contributions to the nation. The film competition became an avenue to explore and depict the various facets of Sukarno's journey, from his role in the struggle for independence to his visionary leadership that shaped the course of the nation.

Throughout the competition, ARMS Production House employed its exceptional talent, expertise, and state-of-the-art equipment to bring their cinematic vision to life. The filmmaking process was a labor of love, as the team meticulously crafted a compelling narrative, employing captivating visuals and evocative storytelling techniques to honor the remarkable legacy of Sukarno. Their participation in this competition stands as a testament to ARMS Production House's commitment to producing high-quality films that educate, entertain, and inspire audiences.
